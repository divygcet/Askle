Process

  1. Discovery

    We align on goals, audience, and voice. I ask questions and map constraints.

  2. Research

    Interviews, reading, and sourcing. I develop a working outline.

  3. Draft

    Clear structure first, then style. You get an on-time first draft.

  4. Revise

    We iterate with comments and edits until it’s right.

  5. Deliver

    Final polish and handoff, with assets and style notes where needed.
